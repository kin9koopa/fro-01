    • Seer

    Seer is a Recon. Recon Legend whose abilities are useful in a battle. His tactical, Focus of Attention. Focus of Attention reveals enemies and cancels various actions made by them. His passive, Heart Seeker. Heart Seeker can be used to determine the locations of nearby enemies. His ultimate Exhibit. Exhibit reveals enemies using their weapons or running within its radius.

    Valkyrie

    Valkyrie is a Recon. Recon Legend who specializes in flight. Her passive VTOL Jets. VTOL Jets allow her to fly for a few seconds, and she automatically marks enemies while skydiving. Her tactical, Missile Swarm. Missile Swarm, launches a group of missiles that damage and stun enemies. Her ultimate, Skyward Dive. Skyward Dive, launches Valkyrie and her teammates into the sky to skydive towards the ground.

    Gibralta

    Gibraltar is a mountainous Legend. His tactical ability Dome of Protection. Dome of Protection creates a temporary half-dome shield that can be walked through, but not fired through. His passive abilityGun Shield. Gun Shield causes a shield to form around the upper half of his body whenever he aims down sights; this shield takes some damage before breaking. His ultimate ability Defensive Bombardment. Defensive Bombardment causes a damaging and stunning cluster strike to land on a targeted location.
